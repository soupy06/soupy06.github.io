Why these places?

These places are either my daily go to places or a place that gives me nostalgia. Like Sunrise Mart, Trader Joes gives me a comforting feeling when shopping there. I love sunsets, and the high line is perfect to watch them. I also like the water so going to the hudson river to watch a sunset against the river is nice. Barnes and Nobles is a place where I can concentrate and find new books.
